SpeedRead on the Firefox toolbar

New extensions stay in the extensions menu until you pin them. Bitwarden and other icons on the bar are pinned already.

Pin SpeedRead: click the puzzle piece (extensions button) on the right side of the toolbar → find SpeedRead in the list → click the pin icon next to it, or open the ⋯ (gear) menu for SpeedRead and choose Pin to Toolbar.

After that, the red SpeedRead icon stays next to your other extension buttons. You can also use the shortcut Alt+Shift+S (change it under about:addons → Extensions → SpeedRead → ⋯ → Manage Extension Shortcuts).
